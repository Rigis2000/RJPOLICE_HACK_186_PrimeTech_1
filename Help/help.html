Frequently Asked Questions:

  • How can I provide feedback through the bulk SMS system?

    To submit feedback, simply click on the link provided in the SMS you receive, selecting your feedback. Your feedback is crucial in enhancing police-community relations.


  • Is the chatbot available 24/7 for assistance?

    Yes, the chatbot is designed to be available round the clock. Feel free to interact with it anytime for queries, assistance, or feedback.


  • How secure is my feedback data?

    We prioritize data security. All feedback data is encrypted and stored securely, accessible only to authorized personnel for analysis and improvement purposes.


  • Can I edit or retract my feedback once submitted?

    Unfortunately, once feedback is submitted, it cannot be edited or retracted. This is to maintain the integrity and authenticity of the feedback received.


  • What kind of analysis does the admin dashboard provide?

    The admin dashboard offers a comprehensive analysis of feedback trends, sentiment analysis, and other key metrics. It aids administrators in understanding community concerns and improving law enforcement strategies.


  • How can law enforcement use the feedback to improve services?

    Feedback received is used for continuous improvement. Law enforcement reviews and analyzes the feedback to identify areas for enhancement, address community concerns, and optimize service delivery.


  • Is my identity kept confidential when providing feedback?

    Yes, your identity is kept confidential. We prioritize user privacy, and feedback is collected anonymously to encourage open and honest communication.


  • Can I opt-out of receiving bulk SMS for feedback requests?

    Yes, if you prefer not to receive feedback requests via SMS, you can opt-out by following the instructions provided in the SMS message or by contacting our support team.


  • How often should I expect to receive feedback requests?

    The frequency of feedback requests may vary, but we aim to strike a balance between gathering valuable input and respecting your time. You may receive occasional requests to ensure a diverse range of perspectives.


  • How can I report technical issues or provide suggestions for system improvement?

    We welcome your input! For technical issues or suggestions, please contact our support team through the chatbot or visit the support section on the admin dashboard. Your feedback helps us enhance the system for a better user experience.
